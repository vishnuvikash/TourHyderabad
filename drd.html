Dhola-ri-Dhani

At a distance of 11 km from Secunderabad, there lies a miniature Rajasthani Village named Dhola-ri-Dhani in Kompally, promising a remarkable experience to everyone. One of the popular resorts in Hyderabad, this is an artistic creation by M/s. Gupta's Hotel & Motels this place offers a holistic entertainment package.

With a magnificent entrance, it welcomes its visitors for an authentic Rajasthani experience with various images and motifs that have been combined to create a village-like set up. This place turns into an extremely appealing setting during nights when the entire place sparkle with almost 2000 lanterns illuminating it. Dholaridhani is not only for someone who wants to savour the authentic flavours of North Indian cuisine but equally for those who are looking for a North Indian experience and also for those seeking fun and entertainment. Been popular for film shootings, Dhola-ri-Dhani Hyderabad will captivate you with its beauty and pamper your palate with mouth-watering food. Dhola-ri-dhani is also just 500 meters away from popular Runway 9 Hyderabad This place does seem to present an enjoyable recreational option. A vast pool, pachadev mandir, Maan Manuhar, Takshila, Ghumar jhumar, Kathputli, Jodhpur hut, lakes, lawn and various cottages are a few of facilities offered here.

The cultural activities, especially the folk art and dance are quite popular among visitors. Offering lively entertainment for adults as well as kids, puppet show by the trained staff is being conducted every day at Kathputli, an open puppet theatre. On request, this open theatre can also used for hosting plays and other activities. Another open theatre is Ghumar Jhumar with a capacity to seat about 400-450 persons. Many interesting cultural activities and plays are conducted here. The dhola-ri-dhani timings for cultural programs are from 6 pm to 10 pm. Whether you believe in it or not, it is quite entertaining to spend a few minutes with astrologer at Dhola-Ri-Dhani offering face and hand reading. There are innumerable activities for kids as well. Few like camel ride, jeep ride, horse ride, mini train, etc. will keep them busy. Or one can just relax and indulge in outdoor activities at the grand pool at dhola-ri-dhani.

From wedding reception to fairs to banquets to seminars to dance performances and puppet shows, this place caters to a wide range of activities. The open air temple quadrangle, Panch Deva Mandir, is often used to conducting religious ceremonies, even weddings. If interested in knowing the culture, explore the Jodhpur Hut which displays a wide range of handicrafts, all reflecting the colorful Rajasthani culture.

Restaurants here boast of colourful, authentic Rajasthani theme. To treat your palate, Maan Manhar offers amazing Rajasthani food which is served in the traditional dining style - chowki style. Sangri is another restaurant located at the top floor of Maan Manuhar, while Atithi Restaurant also offers a chowki-styled seating and is a perfect place for hosting dinner parties. For accommodation, a/c room cottages are also available.

It is open throughout the week. Dhola ri dhani timings are from 10.30 am to 10.30 pm. There are many packages from which you can choose - standard packages, picnic package, get-together package, kitty party, corporate staff picnic, etc.
