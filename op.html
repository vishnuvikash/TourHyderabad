Ocean Park

Situated at Gandipet which is 20 km from the city of Hyderabad, Ocean Park is an amusement park that promises each and every one a day full of entertainment and fun. One of the first theme parks that have been opened in the city, Ocean Park is spread across a huge area with two sections; one for water sports and another solely for amusement games.

This family amusement park, Ocean Park Hyderabad offers something for visitors of all age groups. There are varied choices ranging from water rides, kiddies pool, wave pool, water slides and dry rides. Kiddies Pool, 60-Ft High Ride, the Slam Bomb and Bumping Cars are the major attraction of the Ocean Park. One can also go for Jet Skiing.

Dry rides such as Super loop, 60 feet high ride, bumping cars, slam bomb, Brake Dance, Super Jet and High Swinger promises at least a few moments filled with excitement and thrill. A wide range of water rides are Pendulum, Aqua Snake, Crazy Cruize, Zip Zap Zoom, Multilane Ride, Wave Pool, Harakiri,Tornodo, Aqua-Glide, Aqua Trail and Splash are also available. There is also a kids section in the park with wading pool and fountain. Kids also have toy train, helicopters, huge air filled balloon, video games for their entertainment. The wave pool and the swimming pool also have different sections for men, women and children.

Most of the rides are included in the Ocean Park entry fee. However, for some of the rides you are required to pay extra. These rides are Super Loop, Bumping Cars, Rope Way and Bumper Boats.

There are a few things to keep in mind while you are at Ocean Park. For instance, when going for water slides, visitors are required to wear only the swimming clothes. One can either bring their swimming costumes or else buy at venue itself. However, for women costumes only on the rental basis are available while for men it is available for sale as well. If you are coming in your own vehicle then you need to pay the parking charges at the venue. Visitors are not allowed to take food inside the park. Ocean park timings are from 11 am to 7.30 pm, while water games are open only till 6 pm. So if you want to spend the night somewhere nearby then you can book a stay at the Sagar Mahal guest house which is situated close to Gandipet. Ocean Park is easily accessible; you can go by State Transport buses as well as private cars.

Another striking feature of this park is its reasonable cost. Ocean Park Hyderabad ticket price is Rs. 350 for an adult and Rs. 230 for a child. There are also special rates for college and school students. To avail that one needs to bring authorisation letter from their respective college. There is also a Double Package includes Snow World . You can choose any of these Ocean Park Hyderabad packages and spend some time memorable time with your family or friends. For all those who love thrill and adventure, there are other amusement parks in Hyderabad as well. Mount Opera, Snow World and Jalvihar are also quite popular.
